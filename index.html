About SCJ

Who We Are

SCJ is a private entity transitioning into an official non-profit organization, designed to be the central hub for Saudis living in Japan and a trusted bridge for those engaging with Japanese society.

Our Identity

A private entity becoming an official NPO — built to last beyond any single leader or generation.

Our Goal

The central hub for Saudis in Japan and a trusted bridge for integration with Japanese society, workforce, and culture.

Our Promise

Building a cohesive community where every Saudi is supported, respected, and never left alone.

Our Values

What We Believe In

Inclusivity

A place for everyone. We welcome all Saudis — students and residents — without discrimination.

Support

The safe circle. No member is left alone. From work challenges to daily life, we provide a real safety net.

Empowerment

Center for growth. Committed to your personal, academic, and professional development.

Collaboration

Building bridges. Facilitating meaningful connections between Saudi residents and Japanese organizations.

What We Offer

Services for Every Member

Professional Support

Help finding your footing in the Japanese job market, connecting with opportunities, and ensuring your rights and expertise are respected.

Academic Guidance

Clear guidance on admission procedures, visa requirements, and university life in Japan. New students connected with advanced members.

Cultural Exchange

Presenting Saudi culture to Japanese audiences through events, exhibitions, and community volunteering.
